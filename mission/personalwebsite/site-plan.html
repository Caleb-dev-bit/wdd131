Big Back Energy

Site Plan

Caleb White

Overview

Purpose

Big Back Energy is a fitness-themed web app for lifters to track progress and motivate each other through lighthearted “roasts.” It combines gym journaling with a roast wall, helping users track workouts while keeping the community vibe fun and engaging.

Audience

This site is designed for intermediate to advanced gym-goers who are motivated by humor, lifting culture, and self-improvement. Users are familiar with fitness terms and enjoy tracking their gains and poking fun at fellow lifters in good spirit.

Content

Home Page

The home page introduces the site’s purpose, encourages users to log workouts, and provides an intro to the roast wall. It will feature a welcome message, dynamic greeting (e.g., “Welcome back, Beastmode!”), and a quick journal prompt.

Roast Wall Page

The roast wall lets users anonymously post fun roasts about gym habits or workout fails. Roasts are displayed dynamically with timestamps and usernames (optional). Users can submit a roast using a form and see a live feed of others.

Other Potential Features

  • Workout journal / log section
  • Dynamic motivational quote generator
  • Leaderboard or most-roasted list

Dynamic Elements

  • JavaScript form input for roast submission (adds to live feed)
  • Randomized motivational quote on load
  • Dynamic greeting based on time of day
  • Roast filtering (e.g., “show only leg day roasts”)
  • DOM manipulation to inject roast cards
